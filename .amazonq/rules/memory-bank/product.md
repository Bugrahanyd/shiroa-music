# Product Overview

## Project Purpose
SHIROA is an AI-driven music production and exclusive-licensing platform designed to revolutionize the music licensing marketplace. The platform aims to connect music creators with buyers through a professional licensing system while evolving into a comprehensive AI-powered music production studio.

## Value Proposition
- Professional music licensing marketplace with exclusive rights management
- AI-powered music production tools for composers, vocalizers, and mixers
- Streamlined workflow from creation to licensing with integrated payment processing
- Scalable platform supporting both individual creators and enterprise customers

## Key Features

### Phase 1 - Music Licensing Marketplace (MVP)
- Admin track upload with automated transcoding and preview generation
- Track browsing and detailed track information pages
- Secure purchase flow with Stripe integration
- Analytics tracking and popular track recommendations
- CDN-powered streaming with signed URLs for content protection

### Phase 2 - Growth & Studio
- Credit-based system for platform services
- Studio v1 interface for music production
- Producer onboarding and management
- Collaborative filtering and popularity-based recommendation engine

### Phase 3 - AI Integration
- AI Composer: Automated music composition
- AI Vocalizer: Vocal synthesis and processing
- AI Mixer: Intelligent audio mixing
- Studio v2 with AI-powered editing capabilities
- Model training pipeline with labeling infrastructure

### Phase 4 - Enterprise Scale
- Global CDN optimization with SLA guarantees
- Enterprise licensing platform for corporate customers
- Label dashboard with advanced analytics
- Marketplace features for buyers and sellers

## Target Users

### Primary Users
- Music producers seeking to license their work
- Content creators needing licensed music (video, podcasts, games)
- Independent artists looking for production tools
- Music buyers requiring exclusive licensing rights

### Secondary Users
- Record labels managing catalogs
- Enterprise customers with bulk licensing needs
- AI-assisted music creators
- Platform administrators and moderators

## Use Cases

### For Producers
- Upload and monetize original music tracks
- Manage licensing terms and pricing
- Track sales analytics and revenue
- Use AI tools to enhance production quality

### For Buyers
- Browse curated music catalog
- Preview tracks before purchase
- Secure exclusive licensing rights
- Download multiple formats (MP3, WAV, stems)

### For Enterprises
- Bulk licensing for commercial projects
- Access to analytics and usage reports
- Custom licensing agreements
- Dedicated support and SLA

### For AI-Assisted Creation
- Generate music compositions using AI
- Apply AI vocal processing
- Automated mixing and mastering
- Credit-based usage model
