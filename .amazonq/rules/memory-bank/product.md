# SHIROA Product Overview

## Purpose
SHIROA is an AI-driven music production and exclusive-licensing platform that connects music producers with buyers seeking unique, high-quality tracks for their projects.

## Value Proposition
- AI-powered music composition and generation tools
- Exclusive licensing model ensuring unique content ownership
- Professional-grade audio production capabilities
- Integrated marketplace for buying and selling music tracks
- Real-time collaboration and studio environment

## Key Features

### Music Production Studio
- AI-assisted music composition and generation
- Multi-track audio editing with waveform visualization
- Real-time effects processing and mixing
- Lyrics editor for vocal tracks
- Transport controls for playback and recording
- Professional audio player with advanced controls

### Marketplace & Licensing
- Browse and discover music tracks by genre, mood, and theme
- Exclusive licensing system for unique content ownership
- Secure payment processing via Stripe
- Track preview and waveform visualization
- Purchase history and download management
- Favorites and playlist management

### User Experience
- Role-based access (Producer, Buyer, Admin)
- Personalized dashboard with analytics
- Social feed and community features
- Achievement system and badges
- Multi-language support
- Theme customization (light/dark modes)
- Real-time notifications

### Analytics & Insights
- Track performance metrics (views, downloads, revenue)
- User engagement analytics
- Producer dashboard with sales insights
- Admin analytics for platform monitoring

### Content Management
- Track upload and metadata management
- Audio file storage (AWS S3 / Cloudflare R2)
- Automated track seeding and management
- Search with autocomplete
- Advanced filtering by genre, mood, price, and BPM

## Target Users

### Music Producers
- Independent artists creating original music
- Professional producers seeking monetization
- AI-assisted composers exploring new creative tools
- Beat makers and instrumental producers

### Content Buyers
- Video creators and filmmakers
- Game developers
- Advertisers and marketing agencies
- Podcasters and content creators
- Anyone seeking exclusive music rights

### Platform Administrators
- Content moderation and quality control
- User management and support
- Platform analytics and monitoring
- Payment and transaction oversight

## Use Cases
- Purchase exclusive music for commercial projects
- Create AI-generated music compositions
- Sell original music tracks with exclusive licensing
- Discover unique music by mood, genre, or theme
- Collaborate on music production projects
- Track sales performance and audience engagement
- Build playlists and manage favorite tracks
